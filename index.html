Tax Rates ARE Going Down:
  • City rate drops from 38.460 to 34.293
  • County rate drops from 37.313 to 33.311
BUT: If your home value went up, you'll likely still pay more
Important: In Kansas, homes are taxed at 11.5% of their value
For example, a $200,000 home is taxed on $23,000 (11.5% of its value)
The Key Thing to Know: Home Values Went Up 46% While Tax Rates Actually Went DOWN 1.85%
