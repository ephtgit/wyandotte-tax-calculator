Important Update for 2025:

Wyandotte County has chosen "revenue neutral" for 2025:

  • Tax Rates Will Drop:
    • City rate: from 38.460 to 34.293 (down 4.167 mills)
    • County rate: from 37.313 to 33.311 (down 4.002 mills)
  • BUT: Your Total Tax Bill Could Still Go Up if your property value increased
  • To maintain these lower rates, services will be cut:
    • Less road maintenance (mostly just pothole filling)
    • Reduced funding for police, fire, and sheriff
    • Cuts to neighborhood services

    • Remember: A lower tax RATE doesn't always mean a lower tax BILL - it depends on your property value
